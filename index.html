Why Join?


Technology Is constantly around us and Having the ability to understand,build and create programmes is a skill that is invaluable in the present day. For many technology is a passion and for most its a thing that is around us influecnig our lives one way or another. It is essential and greatly beneficial to have a basic understanding of coding Whether you aspire to be a History teacher or a software engineer.


By joining you'll learn important traits like the ability to Learn,Teach and explain your code to others. Something that is vital to success in the field.
